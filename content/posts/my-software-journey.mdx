## Pre-Highschool

We were poor, had a family computer. It wasn't the best computer
but it was enough to load [Blender](https://www.blender.org) and [Unity](https://unity.com).
I dabbled here and there but nothing serious, just enought to be
considered a hobby.

## Highschool

Throughout this time, I wasn't really focused on much. I slacked
off during 9th & 10th grades. Realizing this isn't going to work,
I put my head down for 11th & 12th. Ended up graduated with a
measly little 3.5 GPA.

While I was in highschool, I met this guy named [Greg](https://unseenthings.net).
He became a mentor of mine, before I really knew what a mentor was.
He taught me how to problem solve. That's essentially what built the foundation
for my skills today.
